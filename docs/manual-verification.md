# Mandatory manual verification

The engine must create unknowns or action items instead of fabricating live insurance quotes, marina waitlists, HOA reserves or minutes, CLUE reports, exact property broadband, rush-hour hospital travel time, neighborhood feel, noise, drainage history, inspections, title/permit history, or ambiguous political and cultural fit.

Milestone 1 operates at town level. These checks become blocking inputs in the relevant later neighborhood and property workflows.
